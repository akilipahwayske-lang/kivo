
# KIVO — Knowledge, Innovation, Vision, Optimization
### Immersive AI Consultancy Website

---

## Brand & Design System
- **Theme**: Dark futuristic + Afrofuturistic fusion
- **Colors**: Deep dark backgrounds (#0a0a0f), electric cyan/blue accents, warm gold/amber highlights inspired by Kenya
- **Patterns**: Subtle geometric African motifs as section dividers and background textures
- **Typography**: Bold, clean sans-serif fonts with glowing text effects

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. **Interactive 3D Hero Section**
- Full-screen immersive hero with a 3D animated scene using React Three Fiber — floating geometric shapes, glowing particles, or an abstract rotating AI "brain" 
- Bold headline: "KIVO — Powering Africa's Intelligent Future"
- Animated tagline revealing "Knowledge · Innovation · Vision · Optimization"
- Dual CTAs: "Book a Discovery Call" + "Explore Services"
- Animated stats counter (organizations served, industries, workshops delivered)

### 2. **About KIVO**
- Mission, vision, and values with fade-in scroll animations
- "Why Kenya?" — Kenya as Africa's tech hub
- Team section with futuristic glowing card designs

### 3. **Interactive Service Explorer** (gamified)
- 5 services displayed as interactive glowing nodes/cards that users can click to expand and explore:
  - AI Strategy & Roadmapping
  - AI Integration & Automation
  - AI Training & Capacity Building
  - Custom AI Development
  - AI Governance & Ethics
- Each card expands with hover/click revealing details, use cases, and a CTA
- Visual connections between services showing how they complement each other

### 4. **Industries We Serve**
- Animated grid for: SMEs & Startups, Large Corporates, Government & Public Sector
- Each with relevant use cases that reveal on hover/click

### 5. **AI Readiness Quiz** (gamified assessment)
- Interactive multi-step quiz where visitors answer 5-7 questions about their organization's AI maturity
- Questions cover: current tech stack, data practices, team skills, AI awareness, budget readiness
- Results in a visual "AI Readiness Score" with a radar/spider chart
- Personalized recommendations based on score pointing to relevant KIVO services
- Option to share results or book a consultation based on the outcome

### 6. **Case Studies / Impact**
- Placeholder project showcases with metrics
- Futuristic card layout with parallax hover effects

### 7. **Contact / Book a Consultation**
- Sleek contact form (name, email, organization, industry, message)
- "Book a Free Discovery Call" prominent CTA
- Location: Nairobi, Kenya 🇰🇪
- Social links

### 8. **Footer**
- KIVO branding, quick links, newsletter signup
- "Built in Kenya 🇰🇪 for Africa" badge

---

## Scroll-Driven Animations (throughout)
- Sections fade and slide in as user scrolls
- Elements scale up, glow, or transform on scroll
- Parallax depth effects on background elements
- Progress indicator showing scroll position
- Smooth section transitions with cinematic feel

---

## Technical Stack (all Lovable-compatible)
- **React + Vite + TypeScript + Tailwind** — core framework
- **React Three Fiber + Three.js** — 3D hero scene
- **CSS animations + Intersection Observer** — scroll-driven animations
- **Recharts** — radar chart for AI readiness score
- **No backend needed** — all static/client-side for prototype
- **Fully responsive** — mobile-first design
